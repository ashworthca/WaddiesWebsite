Age Suitability

Waddies is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. This rating is based on:

  • No violent content
  • No inappropriate language
  • No in-app purchases
  • No chat or messaging features
  • No user-generated content
  • Simple, intuitive scoring interface

App Features

Waddies is a scoring and tournament management app for the physical target game Waddies. The app includes:

  • Score tracking
  • Tournament organization
  • Player statistics
  • Game history

Parental Guidance

While the app is suitable for all ages, younger children may need assistance with:

  • Understanding game rules
  • Managing tournament brackets
  • Interpreting statistics
